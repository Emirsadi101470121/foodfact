How It Works

Choose one card from each food category below: Dairy, Snacks, Vegetables, and Carbohydrates. After making your selections, scroll down and press the "LOCK IN!" button.

A new section will appear showing your selected cards along with interesting food facts. Click on each card to view up to 10 fun facts about that item.

When you're done, hit the Refresh button to start over and try a new combination!
